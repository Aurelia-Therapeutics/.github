## About

**Aurelia Therapeutics** is a clinical-stage pharmaceutical company headquartered in **Woburn, Massachusetts**, dedicated to discovering and developing novel antibiotics for patients with **nontuberculous mycobacterial (NTM) infections**. NTM lung disease is a chronic, progressive, and often debilitating condition with limited treatment options and growing resistance to existing regimens. Our mission is to close that gap with differentiated small-molecule therapies built on modern medicinal chemistry and translational microbiology.

## Our Focus: NTM Infections

NTM infections — caused primarily by *Mycobacterium avium* complex (MAC) and *Mycobacterium abscessus* — affect a growing population of patients worldwide, particularly those with underlying lung conditions such as bronchiectasis, COPD, and cystic fibrosis. Current standard-of-care regimens require prolonged multi-drug therapy that is poorly tolerated and frequently fails to achieve durable cure. Aurelia is building a pipeline of potent, well-tolerated agents designed to improve outcomes for these patients.

## Research & Development

Our R&D program integrates target-based discovery, phenotypic screening against clinically relevant NTM strains, and rigorous in vitro and in vivo pharmacology. Standard operating procedures govern every step — from eukaryotic cell culture and MIC determination through PK/PD modeling — ensuring reproducibility and regulatory readiness.
